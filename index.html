Our Services

From intimate gatherings to large-scale events

DJ Hire

Our DJ Hire packages cater for all events. Birthdays, Weddings, Private Functions - whatever your event we have you covered! With our programmed lightshow, quality sound system, music catalogue from all genres/eras and DJ experience, let us make your event one to remember!

Photobooth Hire

Our Photobooth allows you and your guests to capture fun pictures at your event. Memories that will last a lifetime with both digital print or physical print packages available. You will also have access to an online album which you can share with your guests.

Karaoke Hire

Get the party started with our Karaoke Hire. With a collection of over 20,000 songs ranging from old classics to modern day and multiple high quality microphones, our setup is guaranteed to get everyone singing their hearts out!
