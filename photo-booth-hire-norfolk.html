Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our photo booth hire

How much space do I need for the photo booth?

Our photo booth requires approximately 2.5m x 2.5m of floor space, though we can work with slightly smaller areas if needed. We'll discuss your venue layout during the booking process to ensure we can accommodate the setup perfectly.

Do you provide props and backdrops?

Yes, we include a selection of fun props and accessories with every photo booth hire. We also offer various backdrop options, and can work with you to create a custom backdrop that matches your event theme or branding.

How long can we hire the photo booth for?

Our photo booth hire packages are flexible and can be tailored to your event duration. We typically offer packages ranging from 2 hours for shorter events up to full-day hire for weddings and larger celebrations. We'll work with you to find the perfect duration for your occasion.

When will we receive the digital photos?

All photos from your event are uploaded to a private online gallery within 48 hours of your event finishing. You'll receive a link to share with your guests, and you can download all images in high resolution. The gallery remains accessible for 12 months, giving you plenty of time to download and share your memories.
