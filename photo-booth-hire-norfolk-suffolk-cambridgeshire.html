Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our photo booth hire

How much space do I need for the photo booth?

Our photo booth requires approximately 2.5m x 2.5m of floor space, though we can work with slightly smaller areas if needed. We'll discuss your venue layout during the booking process to ensure we can accommodate the setup perfectly.

Do you provide a backdrop and props?

Yes! You can add a selection of fun props and accessories to your photo booth hire. We also offer various backdrop options and can work with you to choose a backdrop which matches your event theme or branding.

How long can we hire the photo booth for?

Our photo booth hire packages are flexible and can be tailored to your event duration. We typically offer packages ranging from 3 hours for shorter events up to full day hire for weddings and larger celebrations. Get in touch and we can discuss your individual requirements.

When will we receive the digital photos?

All photos from your event are uploaded to a private online gallery within 48 hours of your event finishing. You'll receive a link to share with your guests, and you can download all images in high resolution. The gallery remains accessible for a month, giving you plenty of time to download and share your pictures.

Does the photo booth require WiFi?

Yes the photo booth requires a WiFi connection in order for the instant sharing features to work. But don't worry if your venue doesn't have WiFi, we can simply add a 4G dongle to your hire and all features will be available as normal.
