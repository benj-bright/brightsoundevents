Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our DJ hire

How much space do I need for the DJ setup?

Our mobile disco setup typically requires approximately 3m x 2m of floor space, though we can work with slightly smaller areas if needed. We'll discuss your venue layout during the booking process to ensure we can accommodate the setup perfectly and work around any space constraints.

Can I request specific songs?

Yes! We encourage you to share your favourite songs and music preferences with us before your event. We'll include your requests in the playlist and ensure the music reflects your style and taste. Our extensive music library covers all genres and eras, so we can accommodate most requests.

How long can we hire the DJ for?

Our DJ hire packages are flexible and can be tailored to your event duration. We typically offer packages ranging from 3 hours for shorter events up to full day hire for weddings and larger celebrations. Get in touch and we can discuss your individual requirements and create a package that works for you.

Do you provide a microphone for speeches?

Yes, we can provide a microphone and PA system for speeches, announcements or karaoke. This can be added to your package as an optional extra. Just let us know when booking and we'll ensure everything is set up and ready for your speeches.

What equipment do you provide?

Our standard DJ hire package includes a professional PA sound system, programmed lightshow, DJ equipment and an extensive music library. All equipment is professionally maintained, PAT tested and fully insured. We can also provide additional equipment such as microphones, extra lighting or karaoke systems as optional extras.
